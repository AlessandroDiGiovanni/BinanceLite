What is Binance?

Binance is a cryptocurrency exchange platform founded in 2017. As of May 2021, Binance is the largest digital assets exchange in the world in terms of trading volume.

Who is the Founder?

The founder of the company is Changpeng Zhao, commonly known as "CZ", a Chinese developer who previously developed software of high-frequency trading.
